Cyber Security FacTs

  1. Cyber Threats are Diverse: Cyber threats come in various forms, including malware, phishing attacks, ransomware, DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) attacks, and more. Each type of threat requires a specific approach for prevention.
  2. Protecting Information: Internet security is like a digital shield that helps keep our personal information safe when we use the internet.
  3. Strong Passwords Matter: Just like a secret code, having strong and unique passwords for our online accounts helps keep them secure.
  4. Locking the Virtual Door: It's important to use secure websites (those with "https://" in the address) to make sure our online activities are safe and private.
