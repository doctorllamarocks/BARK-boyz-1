Tips and Tricks

    Approach


  1. Strong Passwords: Approach: Create strong and unique passwords for your accounts. Why: It helps protect your personal information from being easily accessed.

  2. Privacy Settings: Approach: Set privacy settings on social media and other online accounts. Why: It limits who can see your information and activities.

  3. Think Before Clicking: Approach: Be cautious about clicking on links or downloading files. Why: It helps avoid scams, viruses, and other online threats.

  4. Think Before Clicking: Approach: Be cautious about clicking on links or downloading files. Why: It helps avoid scams, viruses, and other online threats.

  5. Report Cyberbullying: Approach: Report any online bullying to a trusted adult. Why: It helps stop harmful behavior and ensures your online experience is positive.

  6. Verify Requests for Information: Approach: Verify the legitimacy of requests for personal information. Why: It helps avoid falling victim to phishing scams.

    7. Avoid


  7. Sharing Personal Information: Avoid: Sharing personal details like your address or phone number online. Why: Protects you from potential misuse of your personal information.

  8. Using Weak Passwords: Avoid: Using easily guessable passwords or using the same password for multiple accounts. Why: It increases the risk of unauthorized access to your accounts

  9. Ignoring Privacy Settings: Avoid: Ignoring or neglecting privacy settings on social media. Why: Proper settings ensure control over who sees your online activity.

  10. Engaging with Cyberbullying: Avoid: Responding to or participating in cyberbullying. Why: It perpetuates negativity and can have serious consequences.

  11. Clicking on Unknown Links: Avoid: Clicking on links or downloading files from unknown or suspicious sources. Why: Protects your devices from malware and other online threats.

  12. Delaying Updates: Avoid: Ignoring device and app updates. Why: Updates often include important security patches.
